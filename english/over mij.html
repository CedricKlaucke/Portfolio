About me

I have a few fun hobby's en interests, which I will tell you more about below

Gaming

One of my favorite hobby's is gaming.

The game i play the most is World of Warcraft, I also play in a group of 20 people with whom I play at fixed times 3 times a week

Festivals

I like to go to festivals and parties, especially hardstyle but also drum & bass and metal.

My favoriete festivals/parties are Defqon.1, Supremacy en Let it Roll.

Skateboarding

I have been skating since I was 14, often with a group of friends.

Usually we skate in a skate park and sometimes on the street.

Bouldering

Another hobby of mine is bouldering.

This is climbing a short route on a real or artificial rock (also called a boulder) often close to the ground without the use of a rope or other form of securing.
From time to time I also go climbing with a rope (top roping, lead climbing).

Airsoft

Recently I also started airsoft with a friend.

Airsoft is a shooting sport that can be played in different ways. By shooting biodegradable small balls (BBs) you try to hit targets and / or take out opponents.
